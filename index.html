Overview

Welcome to the Model Nobel Peace Prize,for Retired IAS Officers,where excellence meets global welfare. This prestigious event serves as a dynamic platform for retired IAS officers to showcase their transformative initiatives aimed at fostering peace and welfare across diverse sectors.

Purpose

At the heart of our mission is the belief that impactful change begins with innovative ideas and dedicated leadership. By providing a stage for retired IAS officers, we aim to:

  • Promote Awareness with Training sessions-
    A collaborative knowledge sharing effort to make sure not just few are benefitting. This includes knowledge related to small business & project management.
  • Promote Excellence:
    Recognize and celebrate exemplary initiatives that have made significant strides in categories such as Women Empowerment, Child Welfare, Education, and Disabled Welfare.
  • Inspire Collaboration:
    Facilitate meaningful interactions among seasoned bureaucrats, fostering collaboration and knowledge-sharing to amplify their impact.
  • Encourage Innovation:
    For selection to be fair, we are continuously working on Standard activities and Games to Identify clear victory efforts and results. Empower participants to innovate and adapt existing methodologies, ensuring sustainable solutions that address pressing global challenges.

Key Features

Conference Meetings and Interview Sessions

Throughout the event, participants engage in conference meetings with Current IAS officers, with other retired IAS officers, With other social welfare activists and so on. We are too naive to assess the skills of all the potential candidates. A proper Video Interview of every participating candidate will be recorded. Knowing who is who will be a step towards fostering networking opportunities and collaborative partnerships. Interview & mentoring sessions with expert panels will provide a platform for participants to showcase their initiatives, receive constructive feedback even from the audience, and refine their projects for greater effectiveness.

Interactive Activities

Participants engage in a variety of activities including debates, games, and workshops designed to foster critical thinking and collaborative problem-solving. These activities provide a platform for everyone to exchange ideas, share experiences, and explore innovative solutions to global challenges.

Experimental Segments

A testbed for your social welfare dreams.Our event features experimental playground where respective sample of participants test and refine innovative approaches to address societal challenges. These segments serve as incubators for new ideas, ensuring that initiatives are not only impactful but also scalable. For example, to improve Education globally, we will try to connect to relevant educational institutions or authorities.

Eligibility

For Competition (Retired IAS Officers and Social Welfare Experts):

Retired IAS officers, interested in social welfare, are invited to compete at city level, state level and national level to test and validate these methods of teaching, within the domain of their influence.

It is like, we are searching for the magic wand already created, sharing with them and asking them to change the lives of as many people as they can. Please reach us to understand how you can participate in this round of- Solving Global Education

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I have to use Bitcoin Mixer?

  • Why do I have to use Bitcoin Mixer?

    Bitcoin is not anonymous. Exposing your identity to anyone may be dangerous. Anyone can reveal amount of funds you have and target a cyberattack to steal your coins.

  • What logs do you save?

    We follow zero log policy, which means we'll fully delete any data related to your order after order expiration.

  • What cryptocurrencies can I mix?

    Currently we support only bitcoin. We'll add more cryptocurrencies in near future, stay tuned.

  • What activities constitute the preparation phase of the event?

    The preparation phase includes the submission and evaluation of portfolios by participants, the selection of the top 20 IAS officers, and rehearsal sessions. These rehearsals focus on refining presentation skills, articulation, and preparing for potential questions.

  • Is Bitcoin Mixer free?

    We charge small fee for using our service. It's the lowest possible fee to cover operations costs: pay for mixing hardware and support team.

  • Can i be identified after using Bitcoin Mixer?

    If you've used our mixing service and clean address to receive mixed coins - you're safe!
